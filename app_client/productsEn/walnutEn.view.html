Walnut

Price: 8 sek/100g

Walnut is the product carefully grown in favorable environmental conditions in the territory of central and western Ukraine. Walnut is widespread in organic healthy diet.

Recommendation:

The best way to store walnut is to use paper bags, as well as cans from coffee, tea or a biscuits. Containers have to be dry. If you do the stocks for a few months in advance, it is better to put the nuts in the refrigerator.

Pistachios

Pistachio nuts have a pleasant taste and unique, delicate flavor. Here you will find salted and roasted pistachios. Pistachios - the only product that does not cause weight gain.

Country CA

Recommendation:

The best way to store pistachios is to use paper bags, as well as cans from coffee, tea or a biscuits. Containers have to be dry. If you do the stocks for a few months in advance, it is better to put the nuts in the refrigerator.

It is interesting!

In Turkey, in the city Gaziantel there is a festival of pistachio producers on December 25 annually. The festival is held with the aim of preserving cultural heritage. On a large, festively decorated site organizers hold competitions and quizzes, dancing and singing, and do not forget to treat all guests with the culprit of the celebration - pistachio. At the festival pavilions chefs of local restaurants cook unusual dishes with pistachios, which also all comers are treated with.

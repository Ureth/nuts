Hazelnut

Price: 16 sek/100g

A nut with a nice sweet taste. The quality of the walnut can be seen from its roundness, size and absence of roasting. The diameter of the core is not less than 13.5 mm. Hazelnuts are very useful, they contain 70% of peanut butter, which is perfectly consumed by the human body. Due to the content of polyunsaturated fatty acids, the use of hazelnut in food improves metabolism. It also has a high protein content, in a quantity not inferior to the meat, and much better digested.

Country Georgia

Recommendation:

1. Hazelnut is recommended for people with high physical and mental strain.

2. The best way to store hazelnut is to use paper bags, as well as cans from coffee, tea or a biscuits. Containers have to be dry. If you do the stocks for a few months in advance, it is better to put the nuts in the refrigerator.

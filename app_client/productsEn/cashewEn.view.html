Cashew

Price: 21 sek/100g

Anyone who has ever tried cashews, very quickly takes them into the category of the most favourite nuts - because they are not as hard as, for example, almonds or hazelnuts, have a pleasant creamy taste and are very useful.

Country Vietnam.

Recommendation:

The best way to store cashew is to use paper bags, as well as cans from coffee, tea or a biscuits. Containers have to be dry. If you do the stocks for a few months in advance, it is better to put the nuts in the refrigerator.

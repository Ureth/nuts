Almond

Golden raw almond Nonpareil - California.

Regular consumption of almond nuts has a calming effect on the human, stimulates the brain and contributes to the improvement of sight

Recommendation:

The best way to store almond is to use paper bags, as well as cans from coffee, tea or a biscuits. Containers have to be dry. If you do the stocks for a few months in advance, it is better to put the nuts in the refrigerator.
